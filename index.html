Why do energy work?

Increased awareness

Energy work can guide you to notice more beauty of nature around you. And to appreciate things like rainbows, cloud shapes, rainy days and so on, more than before. You may choose to spend more time in nature, beach, looking at the clouds.

Different choices

Thanks to energy work you may reconnect to your old passion, hobby, that you used to love, and somehow haven't had time for it for a long time. You may start to choose which activities you spend your time and energy on.

Empowering yourself

Energy work can encourage you to feel empowered by seeing how you can support yourself to feel better/different.
